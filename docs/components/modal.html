Modal

Glass dialog with backdrop, focus trap, and scroll lock. Toggle with data-vitrus-toggle="modal" and data-vitrus-target.

Live demo

Modal title

Modal body text goes here. The glass surface blurs content behind the dialog.

<button type="button" class="btn btn-primary"
  data-vitrus-toggle="modal" data-vitrus-target="#demoModal">
  Launch demo modal
</button>

<div class="modal fade" id="demoModal" tabindex="-1" aria-hidden="true">
  <div class="modal-dialog">
    <div class="modal-content">...</div>
  </div>
</div>
